## Use cases

- **Product demos.** Keep video walkthroughs in sync with your UI. Update the config and re-record.
- **Tutorials.** Script onboarding flows with visible cursor movement and keystroke labels.
- **Changelogs.** Generate clips for new features. Configs are committable and reviewable in PRs.
- **CI/CD.** Runs headless with auto-downloaded dependencies. Add `webreel record` to your pipeline.
- **Screenshots.** Capture PNGs at any point in a flow with the `screenshot` action.

## How it works

webreel launches a headless browser, executes your steps, captures frames at ~60fps, composites cursor and keystroke overlays, and encodes the result with ffmpeg. Chrome and ffmpeg are auto-downloaded to `~/.webreel` on first use, or you can set `CHROME_PATH` and `FFMPEG_PATH`.
